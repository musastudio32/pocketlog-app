PocketLog: Expense Tracker & Budget — Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

This privacy policy applies to the PocketLog mobile application ("the App"), developed by Musa Studio ("we", "us"). We built PocketLog around a simple principle: your financial data belongs to you and stays on your device.

Data We Collect

We collect no personal data. PocketLog does not require an account, sign-up, email address, or any personal information. The App works completely offline.

Where Your Data Is Stored

All information you enter in the App — expenses, income, budgets, categories, notes, and recurring entries — is stored only in the App's local storage on your own device. This data is never transmitted to us or to any third party. We have no servers and no access to your data.

Backups

The App's backup feature copies your data to your device clipboard so that you can save it wherever you choose (for example your notes or email). This action is entirely under your control; we never receive a copy.

Data Deletion

You can delete individual entries at any time inside the App. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all App data from your device.

Permissions

PocketLog does not request access to your contacts, location, camera, microphone, files, or any other sensitive device permission.

Advertising and Analytics

The current version of the App contains no advertising and no analytics or tracking tools. If a future version introduces advertising (for example Google AdMob), this policy will be updated before that version is released, and the App's Google Play listing will disclose it.

Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children. As no data is collected, no children's data is collected.

Changes to This Policy

If we change this policy, the updated version will be published at this same address with a new "Last updated" date.
